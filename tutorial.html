Step 1

To begin, you will first open the box and take the contents out. You will find the PortaPump, a charger, and the following accessories.

Step 2

Choose the proper fitting based on what you want to pump up. For example, select the needle attachment if inflating a ball. Screw the attachment on and insert the needle into the ball's air hole.

Step 3

Press the on button located in the center of the device. Select the speed (RPM) and, optionally, set a max PSI for automatic shutoff. Wait until you reach the desired pressure, then turn off the pump and remove the needle.

Step 4

Finally, enjoy using your inflated ball and say goodbye to bulky hand pumps taking up space in your car!
