Our Pumps are as fast as our delivery!

Nobody likes waiting for their products, that's why we make it our priority to get your package shipped and delivered to you as fast as possible. Here at PortaPump, we ensure your product will get to you in two days. With our new method of double shipping, we ship two products to you so if something happens to one, the other will come on time. The best thing is, the product that didn't make it in time will be automatically transferred to a warehouse in that area. Disclaimer: You will only receive one product; two are shipped in order to make sure you receive the product in time.

Return Policy

If you were not satisfied with our product, or your product came damaged, we offer full refunds transferred immediately to your bank account. We also offer an exchange for the possibly defective product, or a test of our Beta Products such as GoVac and PortaPumpV2.

Shipping Costs

Everybody likes quick delivery, but if you order two PortaPumps, we ALWAYS offer free shipping! PortaPumps are great gifts for student athletes, parents, and more! Regular shipping rates are based on location: within the U.S. = $3.00, and outside the U.S. = $5.00-$10.00.
